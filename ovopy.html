OvopyJourney
tablet app

Overview

Receiving an honourable mention in the Adobe + Southwest Airlines Creative Design Jam, OvopyJourney is a curated editorial experience and travel guide.

Our team worked together to conduct research, ideate, and develop the visual design system. I focused on prototyping the Onboarding + Tutorial process, and collaborated with my teammate to complete the Essentials, Activities, and Checkout pages.

Challenge

In-flight magazines inspire about various destinations and local lifestyles, but don't inform about the destination customers are heading to next. As vacationing begins to pick up over the next year, travellers will seek to discover more cultural inspiration and crave curated experiences.

Solution

OvopyJourney is an application specialized for inflight tablets, designed to accommodate pre-existing travel plans, including booked flights and itineraries. By offering curated resources on the essentials of their destination, the app presents the traveller with a personal all-encompassing guide so they can make the most out of their trip.

The Final Product

Onboarding

Scan your boarding pass to automatically synchronize your flight information and itinerary. This allows the app to offer you a seamless and efficient experience without having to enter any new information about your trip.

Learn

A comprehensive outline of the destination’s essentials, including an A-Z handbook, a snapshot of the destination's current weather, as well as a variety of written and visual resources to simplify your travel experience.

Explore

Tailored recommendations for nearby activities and attractions to add to your travel plans. Complete a quick survey to express your interests and fill up your itinerary with relevant things to do.

Eat

Guides on traditional cuisine, from markets and street food, to authentic local restaurants. Discover locations to eat that are close in proximity to your other plans, view top food recommendations, and browse an array of other informative articles.

Customize

View your current itinerary, add new events, and finalize your travel plans. These screens demonstrate details regarding your travel routes, transit information, and places you plan on visiting to help you stay organized and prepared.

Process

System Map

A system map was created to outline the different sections and pieces of information that would be included. Even with the array of resources and options for customizing the trip, our team wanted to ensure that the user would have a simple experience.

Wireframes

We sketched rough wireframes to visualize potential methods of laying out the content.

User Personas

The target audience is a passionate traveller aged 18–45, eager to discover what the world has to offer. By incorporating features including guidebooks and curated recommendations, the design amalgamates all the necessary tools to help any traveller feel at ease, from the anxious wanderer to the seasoned explorer.
