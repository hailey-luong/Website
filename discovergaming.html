DiscoverGaming

Overview

Our team designed and prototyped DiscoverGaming, an app concept that placed as a top ten finalist in the Adobe & Activision Creative Jam, a UX design challenge with over 700 participants.

At the beginning, each teammate led one major section at the beginning, and my main focus was the gamified activities. However, we all collaborated to develop the app concept, create a cohesive brand, arrange the page layouts, and prototype the experience.

Type

Prototyping / Adobe Jam

Timeline

May 29 – May 31, 2020 (48 hours)

Team Members

Sean Reibling & Justin Chiu

Tools

Adobe XD

Challenge

We were tasked with developing an app experience for exploring video games as an avenue to connect with friends and family. Provided with the freedom to decide on a target audience, we chose to design an engaging and intuitive experience to introduce seniors to the world of video games

Solution

Our mobile application presents a personalized selection of games based on the user’s preferences and abilities, which are captured through a series of activities and multiple-choice questions. All game recommendations are multiplayer, encouraging social interaction with friends and family. A host of other features, such as shared schedules and achievements, create a highly engaging user experience. We believe gaming should be for everyone, and DiscoverGaming helps reach a largely underserved demographic in the gaming industry.

Final Product

Abilities Quiz

Skill-testing activities and multiple choice questions are presented to capture the user's experience with hand eye-coordination, reflexes, and other abilities relevant to video games.

Preferences Survey

Users are inquired about their preferences and skills they are seeking to develop. Regardless of interests and skills, our app strives to match every individual with the perfect game!

Scheduling

The application includes a simple event planning system that facilitates social connection with friends and family.
